We guarantee a minimum number of positive responses based on your tier:

Starter 1,000 2 positive responses
Growth 2,500 5 positive responses
Scale 5,000 10 positive responses
Unlimited 10K/day max 50 positive responses/month

If you follow all conditions below and don't reach the guaranteed number of positive responses, you're eligible for a partial refund.

For Unlimited subscribers: The 50 response guarantee applies per billing month. Responses are counted based on emails sent during that billing period.

Positive Response Rubric

We define a positive response as any reply that indicates potential interest or willingness to continue the conversation.

✓ Counts as Positive

  • "Sure, let's set up a call"
  • "Send me more information"
  • "Interested—what's the pricing?"
  • "Not right now, but reach out next quarter"
  • "Let me loop in [colleague] who handles this"
  • "Tell me more about how this works"
  • "Can you send a case study?"
  • "What companies have you worked with?"
  • Any reply requesting more information
  • Any reply suggesting a future conversation
  • Any reply forwarding to a relevant decision-maker
  • "Not the right person, try [name]"
  • "Timing isn't right" (if they don't say "never contact again")
  • "We're locked into a contract until [date]"

✗ Does NOT Count as Positive

  • "Unsubscribe" / "Remove me from your list"
  • "Not interested"
  • "We already have a solution"
  • "No budget"
  • "Stop emailing me"
  • Auto-replies (OOO, delivery confirmations)
  • Bounces
  • No response

The Standard:

Did they engage in a way that could lead to a business conversation? If yes, it's positive.

Conditions You Must Meet

The guarantee is only valid if ALL of the following conditions are met. If any condition is not met, the guarantee is void.

Condition 1: List Quality

Requirements:

  • All email addresses must be valid business emails
  • Bounce rate must be below 5%
  • Prospect data must be accurate (correct names, titles, companies)

If your bounce rate exceeds 5%, the guarantee is void.

Important:

If you add our List Building service or use Unlimited (which includes list building), we guarantee the list quality. Bounce rate will be under 5%. This condition is automatically met.

If you provide your own list, you are responsible for list quality.

Why:

We can't guarantee responses if emails never arrive. Invalid emails destroy sender reputation and cause deliverability issues.

How to ensure compliance (if providing your own list):

  • Verify emails before sending using NeverBounce, ZeroBounce, or similar
  • Remove catch-all domains if possible
  • Remove role-based emails (info@, sales@, support@)
  • Double-check names and company names are accurate

Condition 2: Sending Domain Health

Requirements:

  • Your sending domain must be properly warmed (2+ weeks of gradual sending)
  • SPF, DKIM, and DMARC authentication must be configured correctly
  • Domain must not be blacklisted

If your domain has health issues that cause deliverability problems, the guarantee is void.

Why:

The best email in the world won't work if it lands in spam.

How to ensure compliance:

Warm Your Domain:

If your domain is new or hasn't been used for cold outreach:

  • Week 1: Send 10-20 emails/day to engaged contacts
  • Week 2: Increase to 30-50 emails/day
  • Week 3: Increase to 50-100 emails/day
  • Week 4+: Scale to your target volume

Tools that help: Instantly, Smartlead, Lemwarm, Mailwarm

Authenticate Your Domain:

Required records:

  • SPF — Specifies which servers can send email for your domain
  • DKIM — Adds a digital signature to verify authenticity
  • DMARC — Tells receiving servers how to handle failed authentication

How to check: Use mail-tester.com or mxtoolbox.com

Check Blacklists:

Use mxtoolbox.com/blacklists.aspx to verify your domain isn't listed.

Condition 3: Sending Practices

Requirements:

  • Send all 3 email variations as a sequence
  • Use proper sequence timing
  • Stay within daily sending limits
  • Use a reputable sending tool
  • Do not significantly modify the emails

If you don't follow these practices, the guarantee is void.

Send All 3 Emails as a Sequence:

Most responses come from follow-ups, not the first email. Sending one email and stopping leaves 60-70% of potential responses on the table.

Sequence Timing:

  • Day 1: Email V1
  • Day 3-4: Email V2 (non-responders only)
  • Day 7-10: Email V3 (non-responders only)

Daily Sending Limits:

  • New domain (<30 days): 20-50 emails/day
  • Warmed domain (30-90 days): 50-100 emails/day
  • Established domain (90+ days): 100-200 emails/day

Sending more than this risks spam folder placement and domain blacklisting.

Use a Proper Sending Tool:

Recommended tools:

  • Instantly
  • Smartlead
  • Apollo
  • Lemlist
  • Mailshake
  • Reply.io

Do NOT send cold emails from Gmail/Outlook manually at scale.

Do Not Significantly Modify Emails:

Minor tweaks are fine (fixing a detail, adjusting tone slightly). Major rewrites void the guarantee because they're no longer our work.

Condition 4: Timing

For one-time orders (Starter, Growth, Scale):

  • Allow at least 14 days after the final email is sent before evaluating results
  • Submit refund claims within 30 days of campaign completion

For Unlimited subscribers:

  • Responses are counted per billing month
  • Allow 14 days after the end of billing month before claiming
  • Submit refund claims within 15 days after the evaluation period

Why:

Most responses come within 7-14 days of the final email. Some prospects reply weeks later. Don't evaluate results too early.

Condition 5: Documentation

To claim a partial refund, you must provide:

  • Screenshot of your sending tool showing all emails were sent
  • Screenshot showing sequence timing (V1, V2, V3 spaced correctly)
  • Bounce rate report (must show <5%)
  • Export of all replies received
  • Confirmation that domain was warmed and authenticated

Failure to provide documentation voids refund eligibility.

Partial Refund Calculation

If you meet ALL conditions and don't hit the guaranteed response count, you're eligible for a partial refund.

Formula:

Refund = (Guaranteed - Actual) / Guaranteed × 50% of Payment

Examples for One-Time Orders (Emails Only)

Starter Tier ($500, 2 responses guaranteed)

  • 0 positive responses: (2-0)/2 × $250 = $250 refund
  • 1 positive response: (2-1)/2 × $250 = $125 refund

Growth Tier ($1,000, 5 responses guaranteed)

  • 0 positive responses: (5-0)/5 × $500 = $500 refund
  • 2 positive responses: (5-2)/5 × $500 = $300 refund
  • 4 positive responses: (5-4)/5 × $500 = $100 refund

Scale Tier ($1,500, 10 responses guaranteed)

  • 0 positive responses: (10-0)/10 × $750 = $750 refund
  • 5 positive responses: (10-5)/10 × $750 = $375 refund
  • 8 positive responses: (10-8)/10 × $750 = $150 refund

Examples for One-Time Orders (With List Building)

Starter + List Building ($1,000)

  • 0 positive responses: $500 refund
  • 1 positive response: $250 refund

Growth + List Building ($2,000)

  • 0 positive responses: $1,000 refund
  • 3 positive responses: $400 refund

Scale + List Building ($3,000)

  • 0 positive responses: $1,500 refund
  • 6 positive responses: $600 refund

Examples for Unlimited Subscription ($10,000/month)

50 responses guaranteed per month

  • 0 positive responses: (50-0)/50 × $5,000 = $5,000 refund
  • 25 positive responses: (50-25)/50 × $5,000 = $2,500 refund
  • 40 positive responses: (50-40)/50 × $5,000 = $1,000 refund
  • 50+ positive responses: No refund (guarantee met)

Why Partial, Not Full?

The refund is partial because:

  • We delivered the work (prospect list and/or personalized emails)
  • Responses depend on factors beyond our control (your offer, market, timing)
  • We're sharing the risk, not absorbing it entirely

Maximum refund is 50% of the amount paid, regardless of results.

List Building Terms

What We Guarantee with List Building

When you add list building (or use Unlimited which includes it):

  • Verified business emails
  • Less than 5% bounce rate
  • Accurate prospect data (names, titles, companies)
  • Prospects matching your targeting criteria

List Quality Guarantee:

If our list has more than 5% bounces, we will replace the bounced contacts with new verified prospects at no additional cost.

Targeting Requirements

To build your list, you must provide clear targeting criteria:

Required:

  • Target titles (specific job titles, not just "decision makers")
  • Industry or vertical
  • Geography (countries, states, or cities)

Recommended:

  • Company size (employee count or revenue range)
  • Exclusions (competitors, current customers, companies to avoid)
  • Any other relevant filters

Sign-Off Process

  1. You submit targeting criteria
  2. We confirm the criteria and estimated list size
  3. You approve the targeting
  4. We build the list

Once you approve targeting, the list is considered accepted. If prospects don't convert, that's a market fit issue, not a list quality issue.

Targeting Disputes

We build lists based on the criteria you provide and approve.

If you later say "these aren't the right prospects," that does NOT void the guarantee unless:

  • We demonstrably delivered prospects outside your approved criteria
  • The prospect data is factually incorrect (wrong names, titles, companies)

"They didn't respond" or "they weren't interested" is not a targeting issue.
